Homemade Pizza

Description

A classic Italian pizza with a crispy crust and fresh toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C.
  2. Roll out the dough and spread the sauce.
  3. Add your favorite toppings and cheese.
  4. Bake for 12-15 minutes until golden brown.
