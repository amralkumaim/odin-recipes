Yemeni Mandi

Description

Mandi is a traditional Yemeni dish made of meat and rice with a special blend of spices. It is cooked in a pit underground to give it a unique smoky flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of Lamb or Chicken
  • 3 cups of Basmati Rice
  • Mandi Spices (Cardamom, Cloves, Cinnamon)
  • Saffron water
  • Charcoal for smoking

Steps

  1. Season the meat with spices and cook until tender.
  2. Boil the rice with whole spices until half-cooked.
  3. Place the meat over the rice and add saffron water.
  4. Place a small piece of burning charcoal in oil to create smoke.
  5. Close the pot tightly for 15 minutes before serving.
