Yemeni Green Salad (Sahlawi)

Description

A refreshing and healthy Yemeni salad made with fresh vegetables and a touch of lemon and olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Fresh tomatoes and cucumbers
  • Green onions and parsley
  • Lemon juice
  • A pinch of salt and black pepper

Steps

  1. Wash all the vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Chop the tomatoes, cucumbers, and onions into small cubes.
  3. Mix them in a large bowl with parsley.
  4. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper, then stir well.
